Shinobi skills

Punch & Kick are the first skills owned by a shinobi, These take 2hp, They cannot be sold and they require no cp to use. 

shinobi skills
1) Kamui(neg/trap): Nullifies opponent's next move of immediate next turn, or attack skill right now. 3cp to use. 20xp to buy

2) Rasengan(atk): -2 from opponent's life. 0cp to use. 15xp to buy 
3) Chidori(atk): -3 from opponent's life. 1cp to use. 23xp to buy.

4) Sealing(time): Seals one of opponent's move from his skill set, user will have to say the skill name and the alphabet it represents or call a random one to seal. the skill can no longer be used in that battle until opponent's life is 5 or below(it can be broken thuo). 2cp to use. 32xp to buy. 
5) Slug Wall(def): This divides any damage of any opponent attack which uses cp by 2. This lasts till the opponent's 4th turn after activation. 4cp to use. 36xp to buy.

6) Ritual(gain): This allows its user to switch one of their unused move with one of opponent's unused move. The name of the skills should not be said, but instead the letter of the skills on the players card, you still play. 3cp to use. 34xp to buy. 
7) Voodoo(time): When your opponent gives you damage, he/she also takes the same damage at the end of the turn. This effect lasts till the end of the battle after it is used. This effect cannot occur when you die, cannot redirect damage that is more than 5. 6cp to use. 46xp to buy.

8) Shinra Tensei(neg): Strike out one opponent's move. You use the letter of the skill in the player's card. If a skill which has been used is called, this has no effect. 3cp to use. 21xp to buy. 
9) Rennin Gakido(def): This skill removes all damage done to you in the last turn & gives opponent the same damage, max damage is 10. 5cp to use. 47xp to buy.

10) Occular(neg): This removes all damage done to you in previous turn, max is 8. 3cp to use. 32xp to buy. 
11)Scarecrow: This brings 4 used moves back except for this one. Opponent plays after this move is used. 5cp to use. 40xp to buy

12)Snatch: This enables user to steal an opponents KKG until the health of the player whose KKG has been stolen is below 7. You can use this even if you are dead. KKG stolen can be used now or saved for later. if stolen kkg wasnt used before opponent hp reached the limit, the skill goes back to opponent. +2cp to use. 55xp

14) Chakra Draining Jutsu(time/gain): Take one cp from opponent and add it to yours, occurs at every of your end turns after activation. This effect ends when opponent's cp is finished. 4cp to activate. 34xp to buy. 
15) Double Rashumon Wall(def): Blocks 2 opponent attacks, can last for up to 3 turns. 4cp to use. 35xp to buy.

16) regen(atk/gain): If used as an atk, -3 from opponent's life, if used as gain, +4hp to user. 2cp to use. 30xp to buy.
