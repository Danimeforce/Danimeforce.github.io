*sword Uppercut(atk)(1 hit strike)(slashing damage)- a simple sword skill which the user points the tip of the blade up and use it like giving an uppercut, if this hits give target 3 damage else if it doesnt, if you have a kick skill you can use it. 20xp

*Vorpal strike(atk)(1 hit strike)- a heavy one handed sword skill with a reach that is double the length of the blade and has high attack power. Give target 5 slashing damage, you cant use attack skill next turn. 44xp

*Savage fulcrum(atk)(2 hit strike)-a sword skill that trace number 4 in the air. Give target 2 slashing damage this turn and 2 slashing dmg in starting of targets turn. 35xp
*Serration wave(neg)(1 hit strike)- a single hit area of effect one handed melee skill, a technique meant to impede movement barely dealing damage. Target cant use time skill next turn, if this skill was neglected, give target 2 slashing dmg. 25xp

*Sword Block(trap)- neglect a weapon skill that deals 5 or below dmg. 15xp
