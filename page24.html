RAIDS

Raiding is a game mode in which players can go in groups or solo, this is basically entering dongeons and defeating beast for loot. although dying before the raid ends makes the loot void and you get nothing, if its a party it turns void when all of them are dead. also somtimes monsters drop nothing(lol nor vex).  you can use  items in raids but they are limited only 4 items for everyone per raid. monsters from raids also gain all used skill back after every 10 turn, they also have a unlimited normal attack skill which can be used always.

they are diffenrent dongeon ranks, when you are about to go on a raid, the rank of the dongeon will be determine by the total level of the party. somtimes stronger monster appear in lower ranked dongeons. you also pay prism before entering the dongeon(each person pays differntly).
