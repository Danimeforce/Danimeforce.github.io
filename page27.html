CLASSES

After selecting a race, classes are the way your character fights.
below are the available Classes.

CLASS: FIGHTER
This class uses melee weapons.

PERKS---->
Hp and Cp Max boost: +2hp, +2cp
(proficiency means what you are allowed to use)

weapons proficiency: simple melee weapons ,versatile melee weapons.
Techniques: radomly get 2 techniques from Acrobatics,Animal handling,Athlectics,History, Insight,Intimidation,perception and survival.

FIGHTING STYLE
You adopt a particular style of fighting as your speciality. Choose one of the following options/perks, you cant take a fighting style option more than once, even if you later get to choose again.

1*Deuling -- when you are weilding a melee weapon in one hand and non in the other, you gain +2 damage for all attack skills involving the melee weapon.

2*Great Weapon Fighting -- when you are weilding a melee weapon that is handled with both hands or has the versatile property and a attack skill used by the weapon is blocked or doesnt do damage, you can make a second attack (this perk can only be used once per turn).

3*Protection -- when a opponent you can see or you can damage at that moment attacks your ally or another opponent you can reduce that damage by 2, but you wont be able to use def or neg skills the next turn after this is used.

4*Dual weilding -- when using two melee weapons at same time, and you use both melee weapons to attack in a turn, you can add half your hp and add it to the second attack damage, you hp divides by half.

5*Second Wind -- once per battle, you can increase your hp by half your cp, max hp that can be gained is 5. your cp is not affected.
