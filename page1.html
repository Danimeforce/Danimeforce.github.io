Rules/How To Play.

 How To Start?

when you get in the group the first thing you do is ask a admin for a character card. you will be asked some questions and given some options, you can get the options from other tabs in the site, e.g races and classes.

 What Is A Character Card?

a character card shows your skills and details.. its basically your game identity, you are advice to not send your card to other players. in the character card they are actions called skills which are to be used in battle, each skill with their own effects.

 Skills and Attack type?

SKILLS

When you have gotten a character card, the actions or the skills(except KKGs) which are on your card can be found here. Skills can be aquired using xPs. The xPs of skills are stated at the end of the context.
they are classes of skills;
melee skills, ranged skills, physical(unarmed and weapon skill)skills.
TYPES OF SKILLS
There are 6 different type of skills the skill type is stated in a bracket after the name of the skill. The 6 types are as follows;

(trap)Trap Skill: these are skills that activate when a certain condition is met either in opponents or users turn although user can choose to activate it or not. they are somtimes called reation or bonus actions.

(atk) Attacking Skill: these type of skills mainly deal damage to opponents

(time) Time Skill: these are skills that take more than one turn to fufll their purpose

(def) Defensive Skill: these are skills that prevent you from losing hp such as sheild ,barriers etc

(neg) Neglecting Skill: these skills stop effect of other skills based on the condition

(gain) Gain Skill: these types of skill increase your stats, hp or cp or even allow you play more than once.

attack types are
unarmed attacks: when you use skills that dont use weapons and also dont use cp. this is regarded as a physical attack cause it doesnt use cp
melee attack: when you use skills that require melee weapons but doesnt use cp. this is regarded as a physical attact cause it doesnt use cp.
ranged attack: when you use skills that require ranged weapons but doesnt use cp. this is regarded as a physical attack cause it doesnt use cp.
charged unarmed attacks: when you use skills that dont use weapons but uses cp. this is regarded as a non physical attack cause it uses cp
charged melee attack: when you use skills that require melee weapons but uses cp. this is regarded as a non physical attack cause it uses cp
charged ranged attack: when you use skills that require ranged weapons but uses cp. this is regarded as a non physical attack cause it uses cp

they are also physical time skills and others , not only attacks. and direct and splash/ area of effect attack. direct attack targets only 1 person, while area attacks target more than 1.

You can only use a particular skill once, unless it is stated in the description of the skill that it can be used more than once. But some skills take turns to recharge for use again thuo.A skill can be wasted, which means, if the move is used in a wrong situation, the move is counted but will have no effect in the battle.

A skill slot is a free zone in your card that skills can be kept.
Note: A skill slot can only hold one skill, but when the slot holds a mode, it can contain 3 modes.If a turn is skipped you lose 2hp but gain 2cp.To use a skill against a player, will be explained later on.

players can sell skills for half the XPs it is worth. they are also skills that require certain weapons to be able to use them

Note if your hands are cut off you wont be able to use skills that requires hand, the admin will be the judge of such skills. Physical skills are skills like punch ,kick, non enchanted weapon skills and so on that don’t use cp.

Buying ,selling and trading.

the currency in this game are xp, prism, gold, silver, bronze. you can also trade other items for items. xps are used to acquire skills, prism and others are used for buying or building/crafting equipment or items. to do any of this you message an admin . you can also buy xp and prism with money (check get more resources).

Hp, cp, speed and counters

speed rules:
if you have 30 more speed than a target that target cant attack you with physical moves unless moves that cant be neglected. in addition
if you have 50 more speed than a target, it cant defend against your attacks. in addition
if you have 80 more speed than a target when you attack, you can use an additonal direct attack against that target. in addition
if you have 100 more speed than a target, your attacks cant be neglected by that target and direct attack skills from that target cant harm you.  

Perks and Techniques

Perks can only be used in a battle if you have equipped them in your perk slots, all characters have 3 perk slots. perks are bonus gotten from classes, the more you level up the more perks you get. most perks are like time and trap skills which can happen over time or need a condition before it could apply. perks can always be used as far their conditions are met. although one perk effect  per turn.

techniques are pick ups that a character has learned, they can be only used once per day and when used they are counted as a bonus action or trap skill. techniques have the following effects. 

Acrobatic: when this technique is used, you gain the dodge skill for that battle only (dodge skill can be found in physical skills). can be only used once per day.

Animal handling: when hunting or battling, you can send back a spawned creature that its level is lower than yours and is a living creature and an animal. can be only used once per day.

Arcana: when you use a skill that has spell in its name, you can use this technique and add that used skill back to your card that turn. can be only used once per day.

Athletics: when your skill is about to be blocked by a defense skill, increase your speed by 30. can be only used once per day.

Carrying capacity: not available.

Deception: when you are been forced to use a skill or to do somthing, you can choose to allow it or not. can be only used once per day.

History: when a creature or player is about to use a skill or take a skill he has already used, neglect that action. can be only used once per day.

Insight: a player or target must tell you two of his skills in his card. can be only used once per day.

intimidation: pick a target, that target cant damage you with direct attack skills in their next turn. can be only used once per day.

investigation: when this tech is used, you play a skill, at the end of this turn, you can choose to remove the skill and its effect and switch it with another skill, if a target responeded to the previous skill with a skill, their effect still activates. can be only used once per day.

medicine: gain 2 hp,can be only used once per day.

nature: the turn this used field effects does not affect you. can be only used once per day.

passive perception: you can lock on a target that you cant see or touch with a skill this turn.can be only used once per day.

perception: when another target or player uses a skill, you can use this tech, which allows you to use a skill that will now take effect before the targets skill.can be only used once per day.

performance: you gain 1.5 times the orignal exp gained from that battle, although this skill must be used at the start of the battle.can be only used once per day.

persuasion: you can target the spawn of a player or creature and let it join your side for this turn only.can be only used once per day.

religion: not available.

sleight of hand: when you play a skill as a mistake, if it brings up an augment you can change this skill to another.can be only used once per day.

stealth: you can choose to begin this battle.can be only used once per day.

survival: you gain 1.5 times the orignal exp gained from that hunt, although this skill must be used at the start of the battle.can be only used once per day.

Learn how the game is played and how to improve and strategise

Terms:
Card- This holds your skills/moves. This is what you use in-battle. When you first join, you have a card that has the following moves, punch,kick,kkg,and others. your initial slots are detemine by your race.
slots(a slot can be bought with 15xp, max number of slots is varies with race). Every player has a card, do not show other players your card(except from admins) so your moves will not be predictable.
Skills/Moves/Justu- These are what you use in battle. You use the moves given in your card to fight. Moves can also be bought using xp. There are special moves(KKG) which cannot be bought and cannot be changed when you are given in your card.

Battle- This is a fight between players in the game, there are different types of battles, including one-on-one, two-on-two(tag team), friendly battle, death batlle, beast battle, tounament battle, etc. A battle is won when a player reduces his/her opponenets health to zero.
Health(HP)- This differs depending on your race(more about this in the Races & Villages section). This is an essential factor because if an opponent depletes this completely during a battle, that battle is lost.
Charge Points(CP)- This is the amount of energy one has. This is needed to perform most moves, it also differs depending on your race. If your cp drops to zero during a battle, you can skip a turn and gain 2cp, but you lose 2hp in the process as well. note that ki, chakra and others a summarized as cp in this game
Xp: This is the in-game monetary system, xp is gotten from winning battles & can also be bought using real money.
Crowns- These are needed to level up your character rank, crowns are gotten from winning battles. Increasing your rank increases the amount of health and charge points you have at the start of a battle. has you lose battle you lose crowns, which can make you go down in rank and decrease your already gotten buffs
dying- when you die you have to wait for the number of days according to your rank for you to revive and continue gaming. while dead you cannot not play online battles or download online content like new story mode and other. you can pay 2xp for each day left to reduce your death sentence.
Ranks are as follows

ALL BATTLE TYPES
1-on-1 normal Battle: a normal battle fought in the group,you gain xp and crowns from this and you do not die as a side effect of you hp turning zero in the battle. body parts cant be cut out and no permenent damage is done(weapons and cloths can be affected instead of body parts).
2-on-2 Tag Team Battle: a game mode like the one on one but this time a 2 on 2.
?-on-? Handicap Battle: custom of the p
Fusion Battle: this is a game mode that give crowns but not xp. this mode fuse two or more players together in sets and then they fight each other.
Tournament Battle: a game mode that has stages where many players contest to win a particular prize.
Event Battle: this is a rare battle created by the admin, where players team up to deafeat a fellow foe. you gain great prizes rom this mode.
Beast Battle - this is battle where winner collect the losers beast , although crowns cant be won in this mode , it can be lost. if players without beast battles a player with beast and lose , he dies and user with beast may not partcipate in any beast battle for a week.
bet battle- this is a normal battle mode where players bet with each other under few conditions, no xp or crowns are given in this battle.
Death Battle: in this battle mode ,if your hp turn to zero you die. you gain huge bonus for wins, parts of the body can be permenently lost.
story mode battle: you fight opponents during this mode and try to climb the chamber to reach higher levels. to unlock this mode you must create a group and name it as af story mode , then add the admins. your story mode battle will take place there.
training battle- this allow you to fight opponents that you have deafeted in story mode, you recieve no xp or crowns neither do you lose them in this mode.
Raid battle
custom battle: just set some rules and play.
During Event & Beast Battles, you are allowed to use special gains called items. To use these gains they must be bought before the battle. When you use these gains in the battle, you still play if permitted.
