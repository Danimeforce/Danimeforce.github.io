class skills/spells

mage/sorcerer spells have teirs which shows the level of the spell.

tier 1 spell
*Slant(atk)(1 hit strike)- a simple skill slashing diagonally giving target 2 dmg .15xp

*vertical(atk)(1 hit strike)- a simple skill slashing vertically giving target 2 dmg .15xp
*horizontal(atk)(1 hit strike)- a simple skill slashing horizontally giving target 2 dmg, if you have used slant(skill) and vertical(skill) in your one or two of your previous turns and they hit, make the damage 6. 25xp

*sword Uppercut(atk)(1 hit strike)- a simple sword skill which the user points the tip of the blade up and use it like giving an uppercut, if this hits give target 3 damage else, if you have a kick skill you can use it. 20xp
*Rage spike(atk)(1 hit strike)- a basic weak skill that leaps at the enemy and follows with an upward strike , dealing 2 damage to opponent , if it hits target is pushed back and cant attack you next turn. 25xp

*Sonic leap(atk)(1 hit strike)- a charge type sword skill that dashes toward the enemy to deal a downward strike dealing 3 damage to target and cant be neglected. 1cp to use(this skill is still regarded as a physical attack because the cp does not go to the attack but the feet of the user). 25xp
*Vertical arc(atk)(2 hit strike)- a simple sword skill creating a ‘v’ shape trajectory. This gives target 2 damage at first for the diagonal first strike, after that hits, give target another 2 damage for the second strike. 35xp

*Horizontal square(atk)(4 hit strike)- a mid level sword skill tracing the shape of a rhombus, give opponent 2 dmg when the first strike hit, after that give opponent 1 dmg then another 1 dmg then 2 dmg, each attack hits one after the other. 40xp
*vertical square(atk)(4 hit strike)- a mid level sword skill tracing the shape of a square, give opponent 1 dmg when the first strike hit, after that give opponent 2 dmg then another 2 dmg then 1 dmg, each attack hits one after the other. 40xp

*Snake bite(nom)(2 hit strike)- two left and right swings that target the opponents weapon with the chance of breaking it. Break a weapon with durability of 7 and below. 25xp
*Vorpal strike(atk)(1 hit strike)- a heavy one handed sword skill with a reach that is double the length of the blade and has high attack power. Give target 5 damage, you cant use attack skill next turn. 34xp

*Savage fulcrum(atk)(3 hit strike)-a sword skill that trace number 4 in the air. Give target 2 damage this turn and 2 in starting of targets turn. 30xp
*Serration wave(neg)(1 hit strike)- a single hit area of effect one handed melee skill, a technique meant to impede movement barely dealing damage. Target cant use time skill next turn, if this skill was neglected, give target 2 dmg. 25xp

*Howling octave(atk)(8 hit strike)(B rank needed)- five high speed continuous thrust then cut downward, upward, before another full force upward cut. Give target 5dmg for the first attack ,this first attack cant be neglected, then give target 1 dmg , another 1 dmg , and 2 dmg. 55xp
*Sword Block(trap)- neglect a weapon skill that deals 5 or below dmg. 15xp

*turnaldo spin(atk)(1 hit strike)(spear only)(B rank needed)- the user tosses the spear towards target with enough force that it spins like a turnaldo torwards its target, deal 4 damage to target, you can pay cp to add more force to the throw increasing the attack by the amount of cp added. this skill is still regerded as a physical attack because the cp is enchances the users body not the spear. the spear becomes unequiped after this skill.
*spear block(def)(spear only)- user uses turns the spear around in front be him facing the direction where the attack is coming from, you recieve no damage from a attack skill in opponenet turn.

*Stab(atk)(1 hit)(knife only): user stabs target with the knife if the attack hits Does damage according to the amount of cp your opponent has, max damage is 3,then you can pay 2cp to pull the knife out and have it still equiped, if the attack was neglected , the knife becomes unequiped and target can pay 2 cp to equip it. 20xp to buy.
*Dagger Throw(atk)(1 hit)(knife only): user tosses his weapon towards target, if it hits Does 2 damage to opponent. By using this skill, the dagger uneqiups, to equip the dagger again you need to use a turn to do it.target can pay 2 cp to equip it. 20xp to buy.

*planetary slash(atk)(1 hit strike)- user rotates the spear around gathering more force and then deal great damage to target, when this skill is in play you cant use any other skills, you can skip as much turn as you want, the attack damage is 3 and increases by 1 per turn skipped(skipping does not take hp and does not give cp), you can then choose not to skip in a turn and thats when the attack skill take place, if the damage hit and its 5 or above, you can cut one of the following part from the targets body; rhand ,l hand, r leg, l leg .40xp
*wind cut(atk)(1 hit strike)(ranged)- user slashes the air and forms it forms a curved blade made from air going toward the target, dealing 3 damage to taget ,if the first attack hits and you have a (one handed battle axe)slash in your skill set , you can use it and give target extra 2 damage . 35xp

*timid slice(atk)(1 hit strike)- when target is injured and on the floor, user dashes to target placeing one foot on the targets hand and slicing it off with the axe in his hand. if target recived damage that is half or more than their current hp last turn you can use this skill, user can cut of either the right hand or the left hand and give them 4 damage.
